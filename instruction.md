If not, iterate.
Every component should feel handcrafted.

Every spacing value should feel intentional.

Every interaction should delight without distracting.

The page should not merely explain a converter—it should make users trust it enough to upload their contacts within seconds.
One extra recommendation
I would also add one feature that almost none of your competitors have: a live interactive demo right in the hero.
Instead of showing only a drag-and-drop box, show a tiny animation where a sample CSV row transforms into a contact card and then into a VCF file. It's immediately understandable, visually engaging, and reinforces the product's purpose without requiring users to read long explanations. That kind of product-first demonstration often has a bigger impact on perceived quality than adding more decorative design elements.
